About the coach

Not just a program — a partnership

I started powerlifting in 2016 after years of aimless gym training. The sport changed everything: structure, purpose, and a community unlike any other. After competing at nationals multiple times, I realized my bigger passion was helping other lifters find their potential.

Every athlete I work with gets a program built around their body, their life, and their goals — not a template. I believe in coaching the whole person, not just the numbers on the bar.
